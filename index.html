Top Attractions in Goa

Calangute Beach

Known as the "Queen of Beaches," Calangute is the largest beach in North Goa stretching 7 kilometers long with golden sands and water sports.

Read More

Chapora Fort

Made famous by the Bollywood movie 'Dil Chahta Hai,' this 17th-century Portuguese fort offers breathtaking sunset views.

Read More

Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage Site housing the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier, this is one of India's most revered churches.

Read More

Dudhsagar Falls

Meaning "Sea of Milk," this four-tiered waterfall on the Mandovi River is one of India's tallest and most spectacular waterfalls.

Read More

Fontainhas

The Latin Quarter of Panjim with colorful Portuguese-style houses, art galleries, and the famous Confeitaria 31 de Janeiro bakery.

Read More

Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary

Situated on the island of Chorao along the Mandovi River, this mangrove ecosystem is home to diverse bird species including kingfishers and eagles.

Read More

Feni & Nightlife

Cultural Heritage

Christian Legacy
